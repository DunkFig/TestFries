fancy fries
This piece is intened for two people in a pitch black room, with the windows closed, and the volume turned up to its maximum.

One Person will naviagate the site while the other person will have their head turned away from the computer, so that they can only see the light reflected from an adjacent wall. The person navigating the site must promise not to let the other person see or engage with the content on the page. After the exchange the passive party may only view the page if they agree to navigate the site for someone else.

This piece is not intended for, nor optimized for your phone. In fact I have deliberately made this site inaccessible to those trying to reach it by mobile.

I'm aware I can't enforce this in any capacity, but if you do choose to view the site this way I promise it will be more engaging, and more directly tap into the ether that I'm trying to reach.

This piece contains loud music, and flashing lights.
